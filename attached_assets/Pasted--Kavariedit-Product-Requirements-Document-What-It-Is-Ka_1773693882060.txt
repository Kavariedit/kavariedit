# Kavariedit - Product Requirements Document

## What It Is
Kavariedit is a SaaS platform that helps anyone create and sell their first digital products without design skills, camera confidence, or marketing expertise. It combines ready-to-customize templates, AI-powered avatar and voice cloning tools, and a guided sprint system that takes users from "I have no idea what to make" to "I just made my first sale" in a structured 14-day path. The platform eliminates the paralysis of starting by providing trending niche insights, done-for-you templates, and faceless content creation tools.

## Target User
**Sarah, 31, stay-at-home mom of two toddlers.**

Her day: She wakes at 6am to crying, spends the morning managing snacks and meltdowns, and finally gets 90 minutes of naptime "freedom" at 1pm. She opens her laptop with good intentions but freezes. She has 47 browser tabs open—"best digital products to sell 2024," "Canva tutorial for beginners," "how to start Etsy shop"—and three courses she bought at 2am during a motivated moment but never finished.

Her frustration: She scrolls Instagram and sees other moms posting "made $2,000 this month from digital planners!" with gorgeous Reels. She tried to make a Reel once—hated how she looked, deleted it, felt embarrassed for days. She opened Canva, stared at the blank canvas, and closed it. She attempted to set up an Etsy shop, got lost in shipping settings (even though her products would be digital), and quit. She's spent $400 on courses and templates but hasn't earned a single dollar back. Her husband asks occasionally "how's the side business going?" and she changes the subject.

Currently doing instead: Buying more courses, saving more Instagram posts "for later," joining Facebook groups where she lurks but never posts, and telling herself she'll "really focus on it" once the kids are older.

## Core Problem
She doesn't know what specific digital product to create, can't design it even if she did, and refuses to show her face on camera—so she stays stuck in research mode forever.

## Unique Value Proposition
Unlike Canva (blank canvas paralysis) or generic courses (theory without action), Kavariedit tells you exactly what's selling right now, gives you templates you can customize in clicks, creates an AI version of you for faceless video content, and walks you through a 14-day sprint with daily micro-tasks so small that "I don't have time" stops being an excuse. The **Brand DNA Extractor** analyzes any Instagram account or Etsy shop and reverse-engineers their color palette, fonts, and content style into templates you can use immediately. The **AI Twin Voice Cloning Studio** lets you record 30 seconds of audio once and generate unlimited voiceovers without ever being on camera again.

## MVP Features (Build These First)

### 1. One-Click Brand DNA Extractor
**What the user does:** Pastes an Instagram handle or Etsy shop URL into a single input field, clicks "Extract Brand DNA."
**What the system does:** Scrapes the target's public images (first 9 Instagram posts or first 12 Etsy listing images), runs them through color extraction API, identifies the dominant 5-color palette, detects font styles (serif/sans-serif/script), and generates a "Brand DNA Profile" card showing: primary color (hex), secondary color (hex), accent color (hex), font mood (e.g., "modern sans-serif"), and content vibe (e.g., "minimal and feminine"). Saves this profile to the user's account. Automatically applies these colors to any template they select afterward.
**It works when:** User pastes "@plannergirlsarah" and within 15 seconds sees a Brand DNA card with accurate hex codes they can copy, and their next template opens with those colors pre-applied.

### 2. Editable Design Template Library
**What the user does:** Browses templates by category (planners, checklists, Instagram posts, Pinterest pins, Etsy mockups), clicks a template, sees a live preview, clicks "Customize This Template."
**What the system does:** Opens an inline editor (built on Fabric.js or similar) where user can: change text by clicking on it, swap colors via color picker (pre-populated with their Brand DNA if extracted), replace placeholder images by uploading their own, and adjust font from a curated list of 8 Google Fonts. User clicks "Download" and receives PNG/PDF. System tracks which templates they've customized.
**It works when:** User selects a "Weekly Planner" template, changes the title to "Sarah's Weekly Reset Planner," applies her Brand DNA colors, downloads a print-ready PDF in under 3 minutes.

### 3. AI Twin Voice Cloning Studio
**What the user does:** Records a 30-second voice sample by clicking a microphone button and reading a provided script out loud. Clicks "Create My AI Voice." Later, types any text into a text box and clicks "Generate Voiceover."
**What the system does:** Sends the voice sample to ElevenLabs API (or PlayHT) to create a custom voice clone. Stores the voice ID in the user's profile. When generating voiceovers, sends the text to the API with their voice ID, receives audio file, provides playback and download (MP3). Limits free tier to 5 voiceovers/month, unlimited on paid.
**It works when:** User records "Hi, I'm Sarah and I love helping busy moms get organized," creates her voice clone, then types "Check out my new digital planner!" and downloads an MP3 that sounds recognizably like her without her recording it.

### 4. Social Content Templates Pack
**What the user does:** Selects a content type (Instagram carousel, Reel cover, Pinterest pin, Story template), picks a template, customizes text/colors in the same editor as design templates.
**What the system does:** Provides 15+ templates per category optimized for each platform's dimensions. Includes "caption suggestions" pulled from a pre-written database (50 captions per category). Exports in correct dimensions (1080x1080, 1080x1920, 1000x1500 for Pinterest).
**It works when:** User creates a 5-slide Instagram carousel about "5 Morning Routine Tips for Busy Moms," downloads all 5 slides as separate PNGs, and copies the suggested caption.

### 5. Trending Niche Radar Dashboard
**What the user does:** Opens the "What's Selling" dashboard, sees a list of 10 trending niches updated weekly, clicks a niche to see: example products, estimated demand score (1-10), competition level (low/medium/high), and 3 product ideas they could create.
**What the system does:** Displays pre-researched niche data (manually curated weekly by admin, stored in database). Each niche links to relevant templates in the library. Shows "Last updated: [date]" so users know it's fresh.
**It works when:** User sees "Homeschool Planners - Demand: 8/10 - Competition: Medium" with three product ideas, clicks "Budget Tracker for Homeschool Families," and lands on a template ready to customize.

### 6. Beginner-to-Sale Sprint Tracker
**What the user does:** Clicks "Start My 14-Day Sprint" on first login (or from dashboard). Sees Day 1 task. Completes it. Checks it off. Returns tomorrow for Day 2.
**What the system does:** Displays a vertical timeline of 14 days, each with one specific micro-task (Day 1: "Extract Brand DNA from one account you admire" / Day 5: "Customize your first planner template" / Day 10: "Set up your Etsy shop listing" / Day 14: "Publish and share your product link"). Tracks completion per task. Sends email reminder if they miss 2 consecutive days. Shows progress bar and celebrates completions with confetti animation.
**It works when:** User completes Day 1-3 tasks, skips Day 4-5, receives an email "Sarah, you're 3 days into your sprint—let's keep going!", returns, and finishes Day 14 with a link to their live Etsy listing.

### 7. Profit Calculator Per Platform
**What the user does:** Enters product price, selects platform (Etsy, Gumroad, Shopify, Stan Store), enters estimated monthly sales quantity, clicks "Calculate."
**What the system does:** Applies platform-specific fee structures: Etsy (6.5% transaction + $0.20 listing + 3% payment processing), Gumroad (10% flat), Shopify ($29/mo + 2.9% + $0.30), Stan Store (5% on Creator tier). Displays: gross revenue, platform fees, net profit, and "to make $1,000/month you need X sales." Allows comparison view across all 4 platforms simultaneously.
**It works when:** User enters "$12 planner, 50 sales/month on Etsy" and sees "Net profit: $476.50/month. To hit $1,000, sell 105 units."

## Data Model

**users**
- id (uuid, primary key)
- email (string, unique, required)
- password_hash (string, required)
- name (string, required)
- avatar_url (string, nullable)
- subscription_tier (enum: 'free', 'pro', default: 'free')
- stripe_customer_id (string, nullable)
- subscription_ends_at (timestamp, nullable)
- created_at (timestamp)
- updated_at (timestamp)

**brand_dna_profiles**
- id (uuid, primary key)
- user_id (uuid, foreign key → users)
- source_url (string, required)
- source_type (enum: 'instagram', 'etsy')
- primary_color (string, hex)
- secondary_color (string, hex)
- accent_color (string, hex)
- neutral_color (string, hex)
- background_color (string, hex)
- font_mood (string)
- content_vibe (string)
- is_default (boolean, default: false)
- created_at (timestamp)

**templates**
- id (uuid, primary key)
- title (string, required)
- category (enum: 'planner', 'checklist', 'instagram', 'pinterest', 'mockup', 'reel_cover')
- thumbnail_url (string, required)
- template_data (jsonb, stores Fabric.js canvas JSON)
- dimensions_width (integer)
- dimensions_height (integer)
- is_premium (boolean, default: false)
- created_at (timestamp)

**user_template_customizations**
- id (uuid, primary key)
- user_id (uuid, foreign key → users)
- template_id (uuid, foreign key → templates)
- customized_data (jsonb, stores user's modified canvas JSON)
- downloaded_at (timestamp, nullable)
- created_at (timestamp)
- updated_at (timestamp)

**voice_clones**
- id (uuid, primary key)
- user_id (uuid, foreign key → users)
- external_voice_id (string, from ElevenLabs/PlayHT)
- status (enum: 'processing', 'ready', 'failed')
- sample_audio_url (string, original recording)
- created_at (timestamp)

**voiceovers**
- id (uuid, primary key)
- user_id (uuid, foreign key → users)
- voice_clone_id (uuid, foreign key → voice_clones)
- input_text (text)
- output_audio_url (string)
- duration_seconds (integer)
- created_at (timestamp)

**niches**
- id (uuid, primary key)
- name (string, required)
- description (text)
- demand_score (integer, 1-10)
- competition_level (enum: 'low', 'medium', 'high')
- product_ideas (jsonb, array of strings)
- related_template_ids (jsonb, array of template UUIDs)
- updated_at (timestamp)
- created_at (timestamp)

**sprint_progress**
- id (uuid, primary key)
- user_id (uuid, foreign key → users)
- started_at (timestamp)
- current_day (integer, 1-14)
- completed_days (jsonb, array of integers)
- completed_at (timestamp, nullable)
- created_at (timestamp)
- updated_at (timestamp)

**sprint_tasks**
- id (uuid, primary key)
- day_number (integer, 1-14, unique)
- title (string)
- description (text)
- action_type (string, e.g., 'extract_brand_dna', 'customize_template', 'external_link')
- action_target (
